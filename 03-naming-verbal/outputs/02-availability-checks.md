## Methodology and honesty note

Domain registration status changes daily. This document records a point-in-time assessment with confidence levels based on name composition and known conflicts. Statuses are:

- **Available** — confident based on name specificity and composition
- **Taken** — confident based on known registrant or obvious competition
- **Likely taken** — common word or phrase; high probability of registration
- **Unknown** — requires live lookup; confidence insufficient to assess
- **Contested** — known competing brand or trademark risk exists

**Action required before finalising any recommendation:** Live domain checks via ICANN WHOIS, Namecheap, or equivalent. Social handle checks via handle checker tool. This document is a planning assessment, not a live report.

## Candidate 1: Pavilion

### Domain

| TLD | Status | Notes |
|-----|--------|-------|
| pavilion.co.uk | Taken | Common English word; venue/hospitality businesses widely use "Pavilion" as a trading name. Almost certain to be registered. |
| pavilion.com | Taken | Taken by Pavilion (formerly Revenue Collective, a B2B sales community). Active registered business. |
| pavilion.app | Unknown | Possible; app TLD newer and less competed. Needs live check. |
| pavilion.org | Likely taken | Common word; multiple organisations likely registered. |

**Suffix strategy required.** Realistic options:
- `pavilioncc.co.uk` — "cc" for cricket club; clean, cricket-native
- `getpavilion.co.uk` — functional, common acquisition convention
- `pavilionapp.co.uk` — works; not ideal (app in domain)
- `pavilion.cricket` — the .cricket TLD exists; niche but fits; check availability

**Assessment:** Pavilion requires a suffix on primary domain. This is manageable — many products operate on `getX.co.uk` or `Xapp.co.uk` successfully. The name itself is strong enough to absorb a suffix strategy. `pavilioncc.co.uk` is the recommended approach if primary is unavailable.

### Companies House

A search for "Pavilion" will return many active companies (Pavilion Business Services Ltd, The Pavilion Theatre Ltd, etc.). These are not in sport/tech and should not create a legal bar. A name like "Pavilion Cricket Limited" or "Pavilion CC Limited" is almost certainly available. Requires live check at Companies House.

### Trademark (UK IPO — sanity check only)

"Pavilion" as a standalone word is likely in use across many classes. Class 41 (entertainment/sports services) and Class 42 (technology/software) would be the relevant classes. A generic English word is harder to trademark exclusively. Risk: low that a single existing mark would block registration, but requires proper legal advice before filing.

**Flag for legal review** if proceeding with this name.

### Proximity to Play-Cricket / ECB

No proximity concern. "Pavilion" is not used as a product or service name by ECB, Play-Cricket, or any major county board. Clean.

### Social handles

| Platform | Handle | Status | Notes |
|----------|--------|--------|-------|
| Twitter/X | @pavilion | Taken | Common word; likely taken by multiple parties |
| Twitter/X | @pavilioncc | Unknown | Likely available |

| Instagram | pavilioncc | Unknown | Likely available |
| LinkedIn | Pavilion | Taken | Pavilion (B2B community) has this |

**Social handle strategy:** `@pavilioncc` across platforms; consistent with domain strategy.
