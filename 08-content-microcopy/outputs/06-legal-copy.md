> Trust badges are based on a club's record of completed fixtures and private post-match reviews. Reviews are submitted by fixture managers after each completed fixture. No review is ever shown publicly. Badges reflect aggregate signals, not individual assessments. The platform does not publish or endorse any individual opinion.

## Age and safeguarding note

*⚠ REQUIRES LEGAL REVIEW — Colts teams and under-18 players*

The platform allows clubs to register Colts teams (under-18 players). Fixture managers arranging Colts fixtures may be adults communicating on behalf of young people. The platform does not currently:

- Verify DBS clearance of fixture managers
- Store player-level data (only team-level)
- Facilitate direct contact between adults and minors

**Flag to Agent 1:** Before MVP launch, the platform's safeguarding position should be reviewed by a safeguarding adviser, particularly around the communication of fixture details involving Colts teams. A brief statement in the Terms of Service may be required.
